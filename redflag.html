RedFlag.exe

Psychological Thriller Escape Room

About the Game

You wake up in a locked room with a perfect girlfriend. Too perfect. Something's off. Navigate through increasingly unsettling conversations to find a way out before you become another victim in her collection.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Antagonist: The girlfriend's behavior, hints, and manipulation tactics are entirely driven by AI
  • Psychological Thriller: Detect subtle red flags in conversations to uncover the truth
  • Multiple Endings: Your dialogue choices determine your fate
  • Adaptive Difficulty: The AI learns from your attempts and adjusts its strategy

The Premise

She knows everything about you. She's charming, attentive, and eerily perfect. But the locked doors, missing windows, and her unsettling comments suggest this isn't a dream - it's a trap. Can you maintain composure, gather clues, and manipulate the conversation to find your escape?

Status: Live on Ripple AI

Platform: Mobile (iOS & Android)
