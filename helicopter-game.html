Helicopter Game

Test Your Reflexes in This Aerial Challenge

About the Game

Navigate your helicopter through increasingly tight corridors and avoid deadly obstacles in this fast-paced arcade challenge. One touch controls, infinite skill ceiling.

Key Features

  • One-Touch Controls: Simple to learn, impossible to master
  • Progressive Difficulty: Corridors get tighter and obstacles more challenging
  • Precision Physics: Responsive controls that reward skilled play
  • Endless Mode: How far can you go?

The Challenge

Test your reflexes and precision flying skills. Each run is different, and every mistake is instant death. Perfect for quick sessions or extended skill-testing marathons.

Status: Concept / Early Development

Platform: Mobile (iOS & Android)
