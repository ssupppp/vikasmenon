About

I'm an AI expert and game executive building at the intersection of artificial intelligence, video, and interactive entertainment. Currently Head of Content & AI at Terra, where I'm building PlotPix.ai — a proprietary AI-native video pipeline that creates TikTok-style vertical content at scale for consumers.

Before PlotPix, I produced AI-native games at Terra — Snackula and RedFlag.exe — experiences where AI drives every player interaction. Before that, I spent over a decade at Zynga as an Executive Producer, shipping CSR2 Racing ($1B+ revenue), FarmVille Country Escape (700M+ downloads), and Mafia Wars to hundreds of millions of players.

I write about AI video generation, prompt engineering, AI-native game design, and production philosophy. I believe in velocity over volume, embracing failure as learning, and treating people with respect. The world is made up by people no smarter than you—so why not take a swing at something new?
