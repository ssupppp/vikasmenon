Bowldem

Daily Cricket Puzzle Game

Wordle, but for cricket

Bowldem gives you six tries to guess a mystery cricketer. Each guess reveals clues about nationality, playing role, batting and bowling style, IPL team, and career stats. A new puzzle drops every day.

Built with vanilla JS and a curated database of 500+ international and IPL cricketers. No frameworks, no backend — just a static site that loads fast and works offline.

Why I built it

I wanted a daily cricket game that actually tested knowledge, not just trivia recall. The clue system is designed so you can reason your way to the answer — narrowing by role, then era, then team — rather than just knowing or not knowing.

Status: Live

Platform: Web

Stack: Vanilla JS, static hosting
