Plumo

Atmospheric Puzzle Adventure

About the Game

Meet Plumo, a plucky little cloud with big dreams. Navigate through atmospheric challenges, solve environmental puzzles, and explore dreamy landscapes in this charming puzzle adventure.

Key Features

  • Physics-Based Puzzles: Use Plumo's cloud abilities to interact with the environment
  • Atmospheric Worlds: Beautiful, hand-crafted levels with unique challenges
  • Creative Mechanics: Rain, wind, and transformation abilities to solve puzzles
  • Relaxing Experience: No timers, no pressure - just pure puzzle-solving joy

The Vision

Plumo is designed to be accessible yet challenging, combining intuitive touch controls with cleverly designed puzzles that reward creative thinking. Perfect for both casual players and puzzle enthusiasts.

Status: In Development

Platform: Mobile (iOS & Android)
